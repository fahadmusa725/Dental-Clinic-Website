Research

Research & Latest Findings

Leading the Future of Dental
Care and Innovation

At 32 Smile, we are dedicated to pioneering advancements in dental research and practice. Our foundational scientific research explores breakthroughs in oral health, regenerative dentistry, and innovative treatment methodologies. Through clinical research, we focus on cutting-edge solutions, including dental implants, periodontitis treatments, and pain management therapies. Additionally, our population health and health services research integrates AI-driven diagnostics, oral epidemiology, and global health initiatives to bridge disparities in dental care. Our commitment is to provide world-class expertise and groundbreaking discoveries to ensure healthier smiles for all.
