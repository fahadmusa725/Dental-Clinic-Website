Case Study

Introduction

Welcome to our dental case study section. Here, we present real-life cases showcasing various dental treatments and their outcomes. These cases highlight common dental problems, diagnoses, treatment plans, and results to help patients and professionals understand different dental procedures.

Case 1:
Teeth Alignment (Braces Treatment)


Background: Ali, a 16-year-old, was embarrassed due to misaligned teeth.

Symptoms: Overlapping lower teeth, protruding upper teeth.

Diagnosis: X-ray confirmed Class II malocclusion.

Treatment Plan: Metal braces for 12 months, with monthly check-ups.

Results: Straight teeth, improved confidence.

Case 2:
Teeth Whitening


Background: Ayesha suffered from yellowing teeth due to coffee and smoking.

Symptoms: Teeth stains and dullness.

Diagnosis: Surface-level stains, treatable with laser whitening.

Treatment Plan: One laser whitening session, fluoride treatment.

Results: Brighter teeth, enhanced smile.

Case 3:
Tooth Implant


Background: Salman lost his front tooth in an accident.

Symptoms: Difficulty chewing, loss of confidence.

Diagnosis: CBCT scan confirmed suitability for an implant.

Treatment Plan: Titanium implant with a ceramic crown after 3 months.

Results: Natural-looking replacement tooth.

Case 4:
Invisalign (Invisible Braces)


Background: Sara wanted to straighten her teeth without metal braces.

Symptoms: Crooked teeth, mild bite misalignment.

Diagnosis: 3D scan confirmed she was suitable for Invisalign.

Treatment Plan: Custom aligners changed every 2 weeks for 8 months.

Results: Perfectly straight teeth without noticeable braces.

Case 5:
Root Canal Treatment


Background: Ahmed, a 40-year-old, had severe tooth pain.

Symptoms: Extreme pain in a molar, sensitivity to hot and cold.

Diagnosis: X-ray showed an infected pulp inside the tooth.

Treatment Plan: Root canal therapy to remove infection, followed by a crown.

Results: Pain relief, saved natural tooth.

Case 6:
Gum Disease Treatment


Background: Farhan noticed bleeding gums and bad breath.

Symptoms: Swollen, bleeding gums, loose teeth.

Diagnosis: Advanced gum disease (Periodontitis) due to poor oral hygiene.

Treatment Plan: Deep cleaning (Scaling & Root Planing), antibiotics.

Results: Healthy gums, no more bleeding, improved breath.
