Frequently Asked Questions

What is dental hygiene?

Dental hygiene refers to the practice of keeping your mouth clean and free of disease by regular brushing, flossing, and professional cleanings.

How often should I visit the dentist?

It is recommended to visit the dentist at least twice a year for routine checkups and cleanings.

What are the best practices for oral care?

Brushing twice a day, flossing daily, using mouthwash, and maintaining a healthy diet are key practices for good oral care.
