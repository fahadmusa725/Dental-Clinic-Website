📌 Frequently Asked Questions

🎓 Student FAQs

✅ You can enroll in courses by visiting the Professional Education section and selecting the course you are interested in.
✅ Faculty members’ details are available in the Faculty section. You can check their profiles and email them for academic queries.

🦷 Patient FAQs

✅ Brush twice a day, floss regularly, and visit a dentist every 6 months for a check-up.
✅ Rinse with warm salt water, take a pain reliever, and schedule a visit with a dentist as soon as possible.
✅ Sugary drinks, sticky candies, and acidic foods can cause cavities and enamel erosion.
